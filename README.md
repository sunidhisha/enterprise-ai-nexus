# 🚀 Enterprise AI Nexus Platform

## Enterprise Digital Manufacturing Platform

Enterprise AI Nexus is an industry-inspired digital manufacturing platform that demonstrates how modern manufacturing systems work together to improve production efficiency, machine reliability, product quality, inventory optimization, and executive decision-making.

The platform integrates **ERP**, **MES**, **SCADA**, **IIoT**, and **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** using modern enterprise technologies including **ASP.NET Core**, **Angular**, **SQL Server**, **Entity Framework Core**, **Python**, **FastAPI**, and **Azure**.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

Enterprise AI Nexus simulates a real-world manufacturing environment by connecting business systems, shop-floor operations, industrial equipment, and AI-driven analytics into a single enterprise platform.

The project demonstrates:

- Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
- Manufacturing Execution System (MES)
- Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA)
- Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)
- Artificial Intelligence & Predictive Analytics
- Executive Business Intelligence Dashboards
- Modern Enterprise Software Engineering Practices

---

# 🏭 Business Challenges

Manufacturing organizations often face challenges such as:

- Unplanned machine downtime
- Low Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)
- Limited production visibility
- Manual production monitoring
- Inventory shortages and overstocking
- Product quality defects
- Delayed maintenance activities
- Lack of real-time executive insights

Enterprise AI Nexus addresses these challenges through intelligent automation, system integration, and AI-driven decision support.
